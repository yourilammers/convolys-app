Getting Started

Quick start

Welcome to our website! We are excited to have you here and help you get started. To begin, you can set up your first meeting. This will allow you to connect with other users and start collaborating. You can also explore our features and customize your experience. We have a variety of tools to help you get the most out of your experience. You can create meetings, transcriptions and summaries/ reporting. Additinoally, you can use our convolys chatbot to ask any questions you want about your own meeting transcriptions. We also have a variety of resources to help you get started. You can find tutorials, FAQs, and other helpful information. We hope you enjoy your time here and find everything you need to get started.

Setting up your first meeting

  • Go to the convolys website
  • Log in to your account
  • Explore the “Documentation” tab
  • Explore the “Getting Started” tab
  • Click on the “New Meeting” tab
  • Enter the meeting details, such as the meeting name, date, and time
  • Add participants to the meeting
  • Click “Publish Meeting” start the transcription
